# Funnel Stage Definitions

## Quick Reference

| Stage | Goal | Audience Temp | Budget % | Primary KPI | When to Activate |
|-------|------|--------------|----------|-------------|-----------------|
| 1. Awareness | Introduce | Cold | 40% | CPM, ThruPlay | Immediately |
| 2. Consideration | Educate | Warm | 35% | CTR, CPC | Video audience > 1,000 |
| 3. Conversion | Capture | Hot | 25% | CPL, Conv Rate | Website visitors > 500 |

## Stage Transitions

```
COLD (never heard of us)
  ↓ watched 75%+ video OR engaged with post
WARM (knows about Sumbawa land)
  ↓ visited sumbawa.estate OR clicked ad
HOT (interested, considering)
  ↓ submitted form OR messaged on WhatsApp
LEAD (captured — hand off to lead-qualifier)
```

## Exclusion Rules

Critical to avoid wasting budget:

1. Stage 2 EXCLUDES: people who already visited website (they go to Stage 3)
2. Stage 3 EXCLUDES: people who already converted (submitted form)
3. All stages EXCLUDE: existing leads/customers (from leads.json)

## Budget Allocation by Phase

### Launch Phase (Month 1)
- Stage 1: 60% — need to build audiences first
- Stage 2: 30% — limited retargeting audience
- Stage 3: 10% — very small hot audience

### Growth Phase (Month 2-3)
- Stage 1: 40% — audiences building
- Stage 2: 35% — retargeting pool growing
- Stage 3: 25% — conversion ads at full power

### Mature Phase (Month 4+)
- Stage 1: 30% — maintenance / refresh
- Stage 2: 30% — steady education
- Stage 3: 40% — maximize conversions from built audiences
