## Content Pillars (rotate weekly)

### 1. Investment & ROI ("Numbers")
- Price comparison tables: Bali $500-2000/sqm vs Sumbawa $8-46/sqm
- Rental yield data (7-15% gross in tourist zones)
- "What $50K buys you in Bali vs Sumbawa vs Lombok"

### 2. Lifestyle & Surf ("Dream")
- Drone footage of West Sumbawa's surf coast: Scar Reef, Yoyo's (WQS venue), Super Suck, Dirty Hippies
- 15+ world-class breaks across 33 km of coastline, all within 1 hour of Kiantar Airport
- Day-in-the-life content: surf, manta diving near Kertasari, island hopping (Kenawa, Paserang)
- "Why I chose West Sumbawa over Bali"

### 3. Legal & Ownership ("Trust")
- PT PMA vs Hak Pakai vs Leasehold — explained simply
- "Can foreigners buy land?" — #1 searched question
- Golden Visa ($350K threshold)
- NEVER say "buy" or "own" — use "secure", "invest in", "leasehold"

### 4. Infrastructure & Development ("Opportunity")
- Kiantar Airport (Poto Tano) — funded by Amman Mineral ($25M), 1,500m runway, the game-changer for West Sumbawa access
- Trans-Sumbawa highway upgrade
- MotoGP circuit impact on NTB property values
- Government "10 New Bali" program

### 5. Social Proof ("Trust amplifier")
- Client stories, before/after builds
- Site visit recaps
- Data: portfolio stats, visitor numbers

## What Works Best (ranked)

1. **Drone video** — land IS scenery. Aerial shots stop the scroll.
2. **Price comparison hooks** — "This beachfront costs less than a parking spot in Sydney"
3. **Before/after builds** — empty land → finished villa
4. **Educational carousels** — "5 things foreigners get wrong about Indonesian land"
5. **POV walkthroughs** — first-person, gimbal-stabilized, show the view
6. **Testimonials** — real buyers sharing experience

## Key Messaging Rules

- NEVER guarantee ROI or say "guaranteed returns"
- NEVER say "buy land" or "own land" — say "invest in", "secure", "leasehold"
- Always compare to Bali/Lombok prices for context
- Be honest about infrastructure being "developing"
- Specific data beats vague claims: "$18/sqm" not "affordable"
