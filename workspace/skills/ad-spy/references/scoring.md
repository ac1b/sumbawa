# Ad Scoring Rubric

## Dimensions

### 1. Longevity (0-10)

How long the ad has been running. Longer = likely profitable (advertisers don't pay for losing ads).

| Days Active | Score |
|-------------|-------|
| 1-3 | 1 |
| 4-7 | 2 |
| 8-14 | 4 |
| 15-30 | 6 |
| 31-60 | 8 |
| 60+ | 10 |

### 2. Engagement (0-10)

Visible social proof on the ad (if available).

| Signal | Score |
|--------|-------|
| No visible engagement | 2 |
| Some likes (<50) | 4 |
| Moderate (50-200 likes, some comments) | 6 |
| High (200+ likes, many comments, shares) | 8 |
| Viral (1000+ engagement) | 10 |

_Note: Many ads hide engagement. If not visible, default to 5._

### 3. Creative Quality (0-10)

| Quality | Score |
|---------|-------|
| Amateur photo, no branding | 1-2 |
| Stock photo, basic text | 3-4 |
| Professional photo, consistent brand | 5-6 |
| Drone footage / professional video, strong brand | 7-8 |
| Cinematic quality, perfect branding, emotional impact | 9-10 |

### 4. Relevance (0-10)

How close the ad is to our niche.

| Relevance | Score |
|-----------|-------|
| Different country / unrelated property type | 1-2 |
| Same country, different island or property type | 3-4 |
| Indonesia land investment, different region (Bali, Lombok) | 5-6 |
| Sumbawa-related or very close niche (surf land, beachfront SE Asia) | 7-8 |
| Direct competitor advertising same area / same buyer profile | 9-10 |

## Composite Score

**Total = average of all 4 dimensions**

| Score | Action |
|-------|--------|
| 8-10 | **COPY**: Adapt this pattern immediately for our campaigns |
| 5-7 | **TEST**: Elements worth testing, adapt selectively |
| 3-4 | **NOTE**: Interesting but not directly applicable |
| 1-2 | **SKIP**: Not relevant enough |

## What to Extract from High-Score Ads

For any ad scoring 8+, document:

1. **Exact hook** — first line or first 2 seconds of video
2. **Value proposition** — what's being promised
3. **Social proof** — testimonials, numbers, credentials
4. **CTA** — exact call to action and where it leads
5. **Visual style** — colors, composition, text overlay style
6. **Landing page** — what happens after the click
7. **Funnel position** — is this top (awareness), middle (consideration), or bottom (conversion)?
