# Brand Rules for Ad Creatives

## NEVER Say

| Banned | Use Instead |
|--------|-------------|
| "Buy land" | "Invest in", "secure", "leasehold" |
| "Own land" | "Leasehold", "Hak Pakai", "PT PMA structure" |
| "Guaranteed returns" | "Potential returns", "historical trends" |
| "Get rich" | "Strategic investment" |
| "Once in a lifetime" | [just don't] |
| "Amazing deal" | "$10/sqm" (be specific) |
| "Cheap" | "Affordable", or just state the price |
| "East Sumbawa" | [never mention — we only operate in West] |
| Lakey Peak, Hu'u, Dompu | [these are East Sumbawa — banned] |
| Any competitor name in ads | [don't name-drop competitors] |

## ALWAYS Include (when space allows)

- Specific price or range ($10-18/sqm, $45K, $95K)
- Plot size in m²
- West Sumbawa location
- New airport reference (Kiantar)
- Bali comparison for context

## Tone

- **Confident** — we know our market
- **Data-driven** — numbers over adjectives
- **Professional** — not salesy or pushy
- **Specific** — exact figures, not vague claims
- **Calm urgency** — infrastructure-driven, not pressure tactics

## Visual Style

- **Colors**: Ocean blues, sandy golds, tropical greens, clean whites
- **Photography**: Drone aerials, golden hour, clear water, surf action
- **NO**: Stock photo feel, oversaturated filters, text-heavy images
- **Text overlay**: Minimal — max 2 lines, large clean font
- **Logo**: Subtle, corner placement — not center

## Legal Requirements

- All Meta real estate ads: Special Ad Category HOUSING
- Indonesian advertising law: No misleading claims
- Must not promise specific ROI percentages as guaranteed
- "Investment" language requires disclaimers in some jurisdictions (AU/UK)
- If mentioning price appreciation, add "past performance is not indicative of future results"

## West Sumbawa Geography (for accuracy)

North to south along the coast:
1. **Kiantar Airport / Poto Tano** — ferry terminal, airport under construction
2. **Kertasari / Pantai Moro** — 15 min from airport, Dirty Hippies break
3. **Jelenga** — Scar Reef (G-Land level), Phantoms
4. **Maluk** — Super Suck ("Skeleton Bay of Indo"), Donuts
5. **Sekongkang** — Yoyo's (WQS venue), Tropicals

Total: 15+ surf breaks, 33 km coastline, all within ~1 hour of airport.
