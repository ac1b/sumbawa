## West Sumbawa Surf Coast

The coastline from Poto Tano south to Sekongkang features 15+ world-class surf breaks across 33 km, all within ~1 hour of Kiantar Airport:
- **Kertasari / Pantai Moro:** Dirty Hippies, Northern Rights, Middles
- **Jelenga:** Scar Reef (legendary, compared to G-Land), Phantoms, Little Bingin, Downtowns
- **Maluk:** Super Suck ("Skeleton Bay of Indo"), Donuts, Playgrounds
- **Sekongkang:** Yoyo's The Hook (WQS competition venue), Yoyo's The Wedge, Tropicals

Other highlights: manta ray diving near Kertasari, islands (Kenawa, Paserang, Gili Balu), waterfalls (Agal, Semporon Tangkel), Ayam Taliwang cuisine.

## Infrastructure Notes

- **Airport:** Kiantar Airport (Poto Tano, West Sumbawa) — funded by Amman Mineral (IDR 390B / $25M), 1,500m runway, 100 ha site. Under construction (57% complete as of mid-2024). Will serve as the primary gateway to West Sumbawa.
- **Roads:** Trans-Sumbawa highway being upgraded
- **Ferry:** Poto Tano ↔ Lombok (90 min by fast ferry, 2h regular)
- **Internet:** 4G coverage in main towns, Starlink available
- **Electricity:** PLN grid in all listed areas
- **Nearest town:** Taliwang (20 min from Kertasari)
