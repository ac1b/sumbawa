# Competitor Analysis — Sumbawa Land Market

_Last updated: 2026-03-02_

## Market State

The Sumbawa land market is **fragmented and unsophisticated**. No one dominates content.
Top Sumbawa seller has 132 Instagram followers. Bali Home Immo has 479K.
This is a massive opportunity gap.

## Direct Competitors

### Sumbawa-Focused
- **Sumbawa Property** (sumbawaproperty.com) — 132 followers, 30 posts. Local connector, $12-40/sqm.
- **Sumbawa Property Trust** (sumbawa-property.com) — claims 15-20% annual growth. No social presence.
- **SumbawaLand.com** — personal broker "Andi", Kertasari focus. No social.
- **Eastern Opportunities** (landsumbawa.com) — 10 years experience, $21K-$45K plots. Has buyer testimonials.
- **Whales & Waves Village** — the strongest competitor. Operating resort + villa sales ($170-350K). Projects 12-13% ROI. Has real social proof.

### Regional (Lombok/Sumbawa)
- **Invest Islands** (invest-islands.com) — 32K Instagram, 15.9K YouTube. Leading Lombok broker. The content model to study.
- **Nagaindo** — South Lombok specialist. Multi-platform presence.
- **Bali Home Immo** — 479K Instagram, 38K TikTok. Industry benchmark for content volume.

### Consultancies (SEO content, not land sales)
- **Indoned** (indoned.id) — heavy blog content, ranks for Sumbawa investment queries
- **Synergy Pro** — "7 Reasons Sumbawa is Best-Kept Secret" ranks on Google page 1

## Competitor Messaging Angles

1. **"Next Bali"** — everyone uses it. Effective but generic.
2. **Infrastructure catalyst** — new airport, MotoGP, government tourism plans
3. **Surf/adventure lifestyle** — targeting Australian/NZ surfers
4. **Hard ROI numbers** — Whales & Waves: $350K villa, 75% occupancy, 12.24% annual ROI
5. **Legal hand-holding** — "we handle everything" addresses #1 fear
6. **Eco/sustainability** — growing angle, 84% of Indonesians choose eco-friendly

## Our Differentiation Opportunities

- **Content quality** — no competitor does professional content. Even basic Reels would stand out.
- **Data-driven approach** — real price comparisons, not just claims
- **Multi-language** — most competitors are English-only. Russian = untapped.
- **Specific over generic** — competitors say "next Bali". We show exact plots, GPS, prices, price/sqm.
