# Content Style Guide

## Writing Tone

- Authoritative but accessible — like a well-traveled friend, not a textbook
- Use active voice: "Sumbawa offers..." not "It can be observed that Sumbawa..."
- Write at a 9th-grade reading level — clear and direct
- Show, don't tell: paint scenes, use sensory details

## Formatting

- Short paragraphs (2-4 sentences max)
- Use subheadings every 200-300 words
- Include bullet points and numbered lists
- Bold key statistics and price points
- Use comparison tables when discussing prices

## Data Usage

- Always cite sources for statistics
- Use the most recent data available (2025-2026)
- Round numbers for readability ($45,000 not $44,827)
- Compare to familiar reference points (Bali, Lombok, Thailand)

## CTA Guidelines

- One primary CTA per article (visit website / contact us / download guide)
- Soft CTAs within content ("Learn more about our beachfront plots")
- No aggressive sales language in educational content
