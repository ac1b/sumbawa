# KPI Targets & Benchmarks

_Update these as we collect real data. Starting values are industry benchmarks for real estate._

## Primary KPIs

| Metric | Target | Alert if | Source |
|--------|--------|----------|--------|
| **Cost per Lead (CPL)** | < $25 | > $40 | Industry avg $20-50 for real estate |
| **Click-Through Rate (CTR)** | > 1.5% | < 0.8% | Meta real estate avg ~1.0% |
| **Conversion Rate** | > 2.0% | < 0.5% | Landing page visitors → form submit |
| **ROAS** | > 3x | < 1x | Revenue from closed deals / ad spend |
| **Frequency** | < 3.0 | > 4.0 | Average times a person sees our ad |
| **Lead Quality** | > 40% warm+ | < 20% | Based on lead-qualifier scoring |
