# Target Audiences — Sumbawa Land

## Buyer Demographics (from market data)

Top buyers by nationality: Singaporeans (22%), Americans (16%), Australians (12%), Russians (~6%), Europeans (growing).
Key segments: solo investors, digital nomads, retirees, hospitality entrepreneurs (surf camps, eco-lodges, wellness retreats).

---

## Meta Audiences

### A1 — Surf Investors (Primary)

**Interests**: Surfing, surf travel, Indo surf, Mentawai, G-Land, World Surf League
**AND**: Real estate investment, property investment, land investment
**Geo**: Australia, USA (California, Hawaii), UK, France, Portugal, Brazil
**Age**: 28-55 (note: cannot restrict age if HOUSING category active — keep as reference)
**Language**: English

**Why**: Surfers who invest. They know Sumbawa's waves, understand the lifestyle value. Highest conversion potential.

### A2 — Expat Investors

**Interests**: Expat life, digital nomad, remote work, living abroad, Bali life, Southeast Asia travel
**AND**: Real estate, property investment, rental income
**Geo**: Australia, Singapore, USA, UK, Germany, Netherlands, Russia
**Language**: English, Russian

**Why**: Already living in or considering SE Asia. Understand the region, lower friction.

### A3 — Property Investors (Broad)

**Interests**: Real estate investing, investment property, REIT, passive income, rental yield
**Geo**: Singapore, Australia, USA, UK, UAE, Hong Kong
**Language**: English

**Why**: Money-first buyers. Care about ROI, price/sqm, yield projections.

### A4 — Hospitality Entrepreneurs

**Interests**: Boutique hotel, eco lodge, surf camp, wellness retreat, Airbnb hosting
**AND**: Indonesia travel, Bali, tropical destinations
**Geo**: Worldwide (English-speaking)
**Language**: English

**Why**: Looking for land to build projects. Our 1ha plot in Poto Tano is perfect for this.

### A5 — Retarget: Video Viewers

**Source**: Custom audience — people who watched 75%+ of our video ads
**No interest targeting needed** — they've already shown intent
**Purpose**: Middle funnel — show carousel ads with property details

### A6 — Retarget: Website Visitors

**Source**: Meta Pixel — visitors to sumbawa.estate in last 30 days
**Exclude**: Already converted (submitted form)
**Purpose**: Bottom funnel — show lead form ad or "schedule a call" ad

### A7 — Lookalike: Website Converters

**Source**: Lookalike (1%) of people who submitted inquiry on sumbawa.estate
**Geo**: Worldwide or specific countries
**Purpose**: Find new people similar to those who already showed interest

## Testing Strategy

1. **Start with A1 (Surf Investors) + A3 (Property Investors)** — highest intent
2. **$5/day per ad set** for first 7 days
3. After data: kill losers, scale winners
4. **Week 2**: Add A2 (Expats) and A4 (Hospitality)
5. **Week 3**: Build retargeting audiences (A5, A6) from week 1-2 traffic
6. **Week 4**: Test Lookalikes (A7) based on converters

Budget ramp: $10/day → $20/day → $50/day over 4 weeks, only if CPL stays under $30.
