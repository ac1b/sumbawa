### What to Extract from EVERY top advertiser found
When scanning (known OR newly discovered), capture:
1. **Headlines that hook** — first 5 words of top-performing ads
2. **Price framing** — how they present price (per sqm, total, "from $X", monthly payment)
3. **Trust signals** — testimonials, "X properties sold", "since 20XX", video tours
4. **Creative format** — video vs carousel vs single image, aspect ratio, text overlay style
5. **Landing page structure** — hero section, CTA placement, form fields, social proof position
6. **Funnel stages** — what's the first touch? What's retarget? What's conversion?
7. **Emotional angle** — lifestyle vs investment vs fear-of-missing-out vs exclusivity
