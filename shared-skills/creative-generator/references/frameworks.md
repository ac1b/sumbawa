# Copywriting Frameworks

## AIDA — Attention, Interest, Desire, Action

Best for: Cold audiences, awareness campaigns.

```
[ATTENTION] Surprising fact or bold question
[INTEREST] Why this matters to you
[DESIRE] Paint the picture of what you get
[ACTION] Clear next step
```

**Example for Sumbawa:**
> 33km of untouched surf coast. Land from $10/sqm.
>
> While Bali plots sell for $500-2,000/sqm, West Sumbawa offers beachfront land at a fraction — with a new international airport under construction just 15 minutes away.
>
> Two prime plots available: 2,500 m² beachfront in Kertasari ($45K) and 10,000 m² coastal in Poto Tano ($95K).
>

## PAS — Problem, Agitate, Solution

Best for: Investors frustrated with expensive markets.

```
[PROBLEM] The struggle they face
[AGITATE] Make it feel urgent / painful
[SOLUTION] Here's the answer
```

**Example:**
> Missed Bali at $50/sqm? Lombok at $100?
>
> Every year, another SE Asian coastline goes from undiscovered to unaffordable. The pattern repeats — and those who wait pay 10x.
>
> West Sumbawa is at $10-18/sqm today. New airport breaking ground. 15+ surf breaks. No crowds.
>
> Request the investment guide before this window closes.

## BAB — Before, After, Bridge

Best for: Lifestyle-focused audiences (surfers, expats).

```
[BEFORE] Where they are now
[AFTER] Where they could be
[BRIDGE] How to get there
```

**Example:**
> You're scrolling through land listings, comparing overpriced Bali plots you can't justify.
>
> Imagine instead: your own beachfront in West Sumbawa. World-class waves out front. $10/sqm. New airport 15 minutes away.
>
> We handle everything — PT PMA, legal, site visits. Two plots available now.

## Social Proof

Best for: Retargeting, warm audiences.

```
[CREDIBILITY] What others are doing
[EVIDENCE] Specific data/numbers
[INVITATION] Join them
```

**Example:**
> Singaporean and Australian investors are quietly securing land on Indonesia's next coast.
>
> West Sumbawa: Bali's surf quality, 1/50th the land price. Kiantar Airport construction underway. WSL surf events already hosted here.
>

## Direct/Data

Best for: Search ads, retargeting, investors who want numbers.

```
[STAT] Hard number
[CONTEXT] Why it matters
[CTA] Next step
```

**Example:**
> $10/sqm. Beachfront. West Sumbawa.
>
> For comparison: Canggu (Bali) is $1,500/sqm. Kuta Lombok is $200/sqm.
>
> Same island chain. Same legal structure. 33km of coastline with 15+ surf breaks.
>
> Download the price comparison sheet.

## Headline Formulas

### Questions
- "What does $45,000 get you in West Sumbawa?"
- "Why are investors looking beyond Bali?"
- "Have you heard of West Sumbawa?"

### Comparisons
- "Bali: $1,500/sqm. Sumbawa: $10/sqm."
- "Same waves. 1/50th the price."
- "The Bali of 20 years ago — but with an airport"

### Numbers
- "2,500 m² of beachfront for $45K"
- "15+ surf breaks. $10/sqm. New airport."
- "10,000 m² coastal land — $95,000"

### Curiosity
- "Indonesia's best-kept secret (for now)"
- "The coast that surf investors don't want you to know about"
- "This island is about to change forever"

### Urgency (without hype)
- "New airport 15 min away — construction underway"
- "Pre-airport pricing won't last"
- "Early investors in Bali saw 20x returns"
